About PACE

Empowering lifelong learning and workforce excellence.

PACE connects education, industry, government, and communities to help people and organizations remain capable, competitive, and future-ready.

About Us

Learning that continues beyond the classroom.

Professional Advancement Continuing Education (PACE) expands access to relevant learning opportunities for individuals, professionals, organizations, and partner institutions.

It serves as a bridge between education and employment by developing programs that support lifelong learning, career growth, and stronger workforce capabilities.

Through collaboration with academic institutions, industry partners, and government agencies, PACE keeps its programs responsive to real-world needs, emerging trends, and changing professional demands.

Our Role

Connecting education, employment, and community progress.

PACE is a dynamic hub for professional growth. It equips learners and organizations with practical knowledge, relevant competencies, and adaptable skills for an evolving global environment.

Who We Serve

Designed for people and organizations ready to grow.
